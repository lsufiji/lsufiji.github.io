Recruitment Team Sets the Scene for a Successful Year

I would like to thank all of those who contributed to this year’s rush, helping us bring in another great class of freshman with the 2018 Pledge Class. As a rush team, we take pride in making sure the young men who pledge our fraternity exemplify the qualities and characteristics it takes to be a Fiji. This year, we bring in a new freshman class with young men from all over the state of Texas. Big cities like Dallas, Fort Worth, Houston, Austin and San Antonio are well represented, as well as smaller cities such as Amarillo, Tyler, Longview, Midland, and Galveston. Our 2018 pldege class consists of members from four states outside of Texas, including California, Ohio, New Jersey, and Tennessee. We are proud of having such a diverse class, with no more than five freshmen coming from the same high school. Also, the chapter is excited to welcome multiple legacies this year who we feel will continue the great traditions and precedents set by our alumni. Thank you again to those who contributed to this year’s rush, we could not have been so successful without you all.
